About Me

Analyst in Progress.
Learning Out Loud.

I'm a junior data analyst currently specializing in Power BI and SQL Server. I'm at the beginning of my data career — building real projects, documenting what I learn, and sharing the process honestly.

ENGR INSIGHT is where I publish my project write-ups. It's not a course or tutorial blog — it's a record of actual things I've built, the decisions I made, what worked, and what I'd do differently.

My current focus is on becoming genuinely good at the fundamentals: writing clean SQL, understanding data modeling, and building Power BI reports that actually answer business questions.
